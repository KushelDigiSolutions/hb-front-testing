About Us

HealthyBazar was born with the idea that healing needs to be holistic and only traditional medicine systems (AYUSH) such as Ayurveda and Unani can heal the body comprehensively. In contrast, all other medicine systems often focus only on managing symptoms, offering placebo effect.

We wanted to make Ayurveda and Unani treatments more accessible. Thus, we brought to you a reliable online platform that not only delivers top-rated herbal medicines anytime but also provides online consultations with highly qualified practitioners at your fingertips. HealthyBazar offers a wide variety of 100% authentic natural wellness solutions designed for different health concerns.

Founded in 2015 by Snehil & Himanshu Singhal, HealthyBazar carried forward a 75-year legacy of holistic healing and trusted care built by their family.

Why We Started HealthyBazar.com?
HealthyBazar is more than just a digital wellness store. It all began after a simple observation - finding trusted Ayurvedic or Unani medicines online felt like an uphill battle. Either the products weren’t genuine or there was a lack of guidance on what truly catered to one’s requirement. We noticed families struggling to get age-old remedies that their elders resorted to and availability of excessive information (sometimes incorrect too) left wellness seekers only overwhelmed and misguided. That’s when the idea of HealthyBazar came to life.

We have built a curated space where traditional knowledge befriended modern convenience. At HealthyBazar, trust isn’t just another promise; it’s the foundation of everything we do. Thoughtful guidance by certified AYUSH practitioners who understand the value of safe and effective remedies, is what sets our online platform apart. We’re dedicated to offering only the best in Ayurvedic and Unani medicines, as well as fresh, natural herbs that support a range of wellness needs. Every product available is chosen with care and comes from top-selling and trusted brands, so you can feel confident in selecting formulations that work the best for you.
